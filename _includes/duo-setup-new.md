Enter your __username__, then enter your newly created __password__, and click [Sign in](){: .btn .btn--inverse .btn--small}

__NOTE:__ Do not select __Sign with FastPass__, this will not work and needs to be set up at a later stage.
{: .notice--warning}

{% include figure url="/assets/images/okta-login-mac1.png" image_path="/assets/images/okta-login-mac1.png" %}

When asked to verify it's you, choose __Security Key or Biometric Authenticator__ and click [Select](){: .btn .btn--inverse .btn--small}

{% include figure url="/assets/images/okta-security-method.png" image_path="/assets/images/okta-security-method.png" %}

On the __Verify with Security Key or Biometric Authenticator__ screen, click [Verify](){: .btn .btn--inverse .btn--small}

{% include figure url="/assets/images/okta-security-verify.png" image_path="/assets/images/okta-security-verify.png" %}

In the __Sign In__ dialog, select __Use Security Key__ and click [Continue](){: .btn .btn--inverse .btn--small}

__NOTE:__ __Scan QR Code__ is selected by default — make sure to change it to __Use Security Key__.
{: .notice--warning}

{% include figure url="/assets/images/security-key-use.png" image_path="/assets/images/security-key-use.png" %}

Insert your __YubiKey__ into a USB port on your laptop and tap the metal contact to activate it.

{% include figure url="/assets/images/security-key-activate.png" image_path="/assets/images/security-key-activate.png" %}

If prompted, set a PIN for your security key (or enter your existing PIN), then click [Continue](){: .btn .btn--inverse .btn--small}

{% include figure url="/assets/images/security-key-pin.png" image_path="/assets/images/security-key-pin.png" %}

To finish, tap the metal contact on your YubiKey once more.

{% include figure url="/assets/images/security-key-activate-again.png" image_path="/assets/images/security-key-activate-again.png" %}
