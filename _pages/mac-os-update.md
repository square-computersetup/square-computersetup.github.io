      <strong>This update can take a while</strong><br/>
      macOS updates often require a restart and may take 15–45 minutes. Run this when you have some downtime — over lunch, at the end of the day, or between meetings. If your macOS is out of date, you won't be able to access some Block internal tools.
    </div>

  <div class="welcome-section">
    <h2>Update your Mac</h2>
    <div class="welcome-checklist">

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">1</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Open Software Update</strong>
          <p>Click the <strong>Apple menu</strong> in the top-left corner of your screen, then go to <strong>System Settings</strong> &rarr; <strong>General</strong> &rarr; <strong>Software Update</strong>.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/macos-update-01.png" image_path="/assets/images/macos-update-01.png" %}
{% include figure url="/assets/images/macos-update-02.png" image_path="/assets/images/macos-update-02.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">2</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Install available updates</strong>
          <p>If new software is found, click <strong>Update Now</strong>, <strong>Upgrade Now</strong>, or <strong>Restart Now</strong> — the button name varies depending on the update. You'll be asked to enter your Mac/Laptop Password.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">3</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Wait for the update to complete</strong>
          <p>Your Mac may restart one or more times during the update. Once it's finished, log back in and you're all set.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="checklist-item" style="background: #e8f5e9; border-color: #c8e6c9;">
        <div class="checklist-icon" style="background: #fff; border-color: #c8e6c9; color: #00a828;"><svg width="20" height="20" viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round"><polyline points="20 6 9 17 4 12"/></svg></div>
        <div>
          <strong>You're up to date!</strong>
          <p>Your Mac is now running the latest macOS version.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

    </div>
