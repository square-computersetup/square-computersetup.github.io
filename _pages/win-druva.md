<div class="welcome-container" markdown="0">

  <h1 class="welcome-title">Druva</h1>
  <p class="welcome-subtitle">Activate Druva to automatically back up your computer to the cloud.</p>

  <div class="welcome-section">
    <h2>Activate backup</h2>
    <div class="welcome-checklist">

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">1</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Open Druva inSync</strong>
          <p>Double-click the <strong>Druva inSync</strong> icon on your desktop.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/win-druva1.png" image_path="/assets/images/win-druva1.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">2</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Enter your username</strong>
          <p>Type your username ending in <strong>@squareup.com</strong> and click <strong>Continue</strong>.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="notice--danger" style="display:flex; gap:1em; align-items:flex-start;">
        <svg viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="1.5" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" style="width:24px; min-width:24px; height:24px; margin-top:0.1em;"><circle cx="12" cy="12" r="10"/><line x1="15" y1="9" x2="9" y2="15"/><line x1="9" y1="9" x2="15" y2="15"/></svg>
        <div>
          <strong>Must use @squareup.com</strong><br/>
          Druva will not work with @block.xyz or @tidal.com. Use your <strong>@squareup.com</strong> email (e.g. jsmith@squareup.com).
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/win-druva2.png" image_path="/assets/images/win-druva2.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">3</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Authenticate with your PIN</strong>
          <p>Chrome and Okta will open, and a Windows Security prompt will appear. Enter the <strong>PIN</strong> you set up earlier to authenticate.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/win-druva3.png" image_path="/assets/images/win-druva3.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">4</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Open InSync</strong>
          <p>When the "Open InSync?" pop-up appears, click <strong>Open InSyncAgent</strong>.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/win-druva4.png" image_path="/assets/images/win-druva4.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">5</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Add as new device</strong>
          <p>The Druva window will return. Click <strong>Add as New Device</strong>. Druva will begin backing up your data.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/win-druva-replace.png" image_path="/assets/images/win-druva-replace.png" %}

      <div class="notice--info" style="display:flex; gap:1em; align-items:flex-start;">
        <svg viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="1.5" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" style="width:24px; min-width:24px; height:24px; margin-top:0.1em;"><circle cx="12" cy="12" r="10"/><line x1="12" y1="16" x2="12" y2="12"/><line x1="12" y1="8" x2="12.01" y2="8"/></svg>
        <div>
          <strong>Replacing an old Block laptop?</strong><br/>
          Still set up as a new device — restoring a full backup can bring over unwanted files. If you need specific files from your old laptop, you can select them individually through the Druva panel later.
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="checklist-item" style="background: #e8f5e9; border-color: #c8e6c9;">
        <div class="checklist-icon" style="background: #fff; border-color: #c8e6c9; color: #00a828;"><svg width="20" height="20" viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round"><polyline points="20 6 9 17 4 12"/></svg></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Backup activated!</strong>
          <p>Druva will back up your computer automatically in the background.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

    </div>
  </div>
