<div class="welcome-container" markdown="0">

  <h1 class="welcome-title">Chrome Setup</h1>
  <p class="welcome-subtitle">Set Chrome as your default browser and sign in with your Block account.</p>

  <div class="welcome-section">
    <h2>Set Chrome as default browser</h2>
    <div class="welcome-checklist">

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">1</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Open Default Apps settings</strong>
          <p>Open <strong>Settings</strong>, click <strong>Apps</strong> in the left sidebar, then select <strong>Default Apps</strong>.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/win-chrome-01.png" image_path="/assets/images/win-chrome-01.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">2</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Set Chrome as default</strong>
          <p>Search for <strong>Google Chrome</strong>, select it, and click <strong>Set default</strong>.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/win-chrome-02.png" image_path="/assets/images/win-chrome-02.png" %}
{% include figure url="/assets/images/win-chrome-03.png" image_path="/assets/images/win-chrome-03.png" %}

    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="welcome-section">
    <h2>Sign into Chrome</h2>
    <div class="welcome-checklist">

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">3</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Open Chrome</strong>
          <p>Open <strong>Google Chrome</strong> from the desktop. If the icon isn't there yet, wait a minute or two for Windows Updates to finish installing it.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">4</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Sign in with your email</strong>
          <p>Click <strong>Sign In</strong> and enter your Block email address. An Okta login page will appear — click <strong>Sign in with Okta FastPass</strong>.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/win-chrome-05.png" image_path="/assets/images/win-chrome-05.png" %}
{% include figure url="/assets/images/win-chrome-06.png" image_path="/assets/images/win-chrome-06.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">5</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Authenticate and enter your password</strong>
          <p>Okta FastPass will verify your identity using biometrics. Once authenticated, the Okta login screen will reload — enter your <strong>password</strong> to continue.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/win-chrome-07.png" image_path="/assets/images/win-chrome-07.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">6</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Accept managed profile</strong>
          <p>You'll see a page that says "Your organization will manage this profile." Leave the box checked and click <strong>Continue</strong>.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/win-chrome-09.png" image_path="/assets/images/win-chrome-09.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">7</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Turn on Sync</strong>
          <p>When prompted to "Turn on Sync," click <strong>Yes, I'm in</strong>. This downloads the required Chrome extensions for go/links and other internal tools.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/win-chrome-10.png" image_path="/assets/images/win-chrome-10.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">8</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Confirm you're signed in</strong>
          <p>You should see your profile icon or picture in the top-right corner of Chrome.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/win-chrome-11.png" image_path="/assets/images/win-chrome-11.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item" style="background: #e8f5e9; border-color: #c8e6c9;">
        <div class="checklist-icon" style="background: #fff; border-color: #c8e6c9; color: #00a828;"><svg width="20" height="20" viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round"><polyline points="20 6 9 17 4 12"/></svg></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Chrome is ready!</strong>
          <p>You're signed in and synced with your Block account.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

    </div>
  </div>
