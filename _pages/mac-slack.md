  <div class="welcome-section">
    <h2>Open &amp; sign into Slack</h2>
    <div class="welcome-checklist">

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">1</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Open Slack</strong>
          <p>Use <strong>Spotlight</strong> (magnifying glass icon in the top-right corner) to search for and open the <strong>Slack</strong> app.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/mac-spotlight.png" image_path="/assets/images/mac-spotlight.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">2</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Allow access to Downloads</strong>
          <p>If prompted, click <strong>OK</strong> to give Slack access to your Downloads folder.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/mac-slack-downloads.png" image_path="/assets/images/mac-slack-downloads.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">3</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Click Sign in with Slack</strong>
          <p>A Chrome window will open. Click <strong>Try entering a workspace URL</strong> at the bottom of the window.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/slack-signin.png" image_path="/assets/images/slack-signin.png" %}
{% include figure url="/assets/images/slack-workspace-url.png" image_path="/assets/images/slack-workspace-url.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">4</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Enter the workspace URL</strong>
          <p>Where it says "your-workspace" in the URL, type <strong>square</strong> and click <strong>Continue</strong>.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/slack-workspace-signin.png" image_path="/assets/images/slack-workspace-signin.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">5</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Sign in with Block SSO</strong>
          <p>Click <strong>Sign in with Block SSO</strong>. An Okta window will open where you can authenticate.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="welcome-section">
    <h2>Choose your workspace</h2>
    <div class="welcome-checklist">

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">6</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Select the Block workspace</strong>
          <p>You'll see a list of available workspaces. Select <strong>Block</strong> — Slack now merges all workspaces into a single interface, so you don't need to add any others.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/slack-workspace-select.png" image_path="/assets/images/slack-workspace-select.png" %}

      <div class="checklist-item">
        <div class="checklist-icon"><span style="font-weight:700; font-size:1rem;">7</span></div>
        <div>
          <strong>Launch in Slack</strong>
          <p>Click <strong>Launch in Slack</strong> for your chosen workspace, then click <strong>Open Slack</strong> when Chrome asks to open the app.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

{% include figure url="/assets/images/mac-slack-open.png" image_path="/assets/images/mac-slack-open.png" %}

      <div class="notice--warning" style="display:flex; gap:1em; align-items:flex-start;">
        <svg viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="1.5" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" style="width:24px; min-width:24px; height:24px; margin-top:0.1em;"><path d="M10.29 3.86L1.82 18a2 2 0 0 0 1.71 3h16.94a2 2 0 0 0 1.71-3L13.71 3.86a2 2 0 0 0-3.42 0z"/><line x1="12" y1="9" x2="12" y2="13"/><line x1="12" y1="17" x2="12.01" y2="17"/></svg>
        <div>
          <strong>Slack didn't open?</strong><br/>
          If Slack doesn't open in the app the first time, go back and click <strong>Sign in</strong> again within the Slack app and try a second time.
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="checklist-item" style="background: #e8f5e9; border-color: #c8e6c9;">
        <div class="checklist-icon" style="background: #fff; border-color: #c8e6c9; color: #00a828;"><svg width="20" height="20" viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round"><polyline points="20 6 9 17 4 12"/></svg></div>
        <div>
          <strong>You're on Slack!</strong>
          <p>If it's your first day, consider messaging your lead to let them know you're online.</p>
        </div>
      </div>

    </div>
